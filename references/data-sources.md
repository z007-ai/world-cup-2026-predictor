# Data Sources

## Source Priority

Use the highest-quality available source for each claim.

1. Official FIFA pages
   Use for:
   - rankings
   - tournament format
   - draw and groups
   - qualified teams
   - squad announcements
   - match schedule
2. Official federation pages
   Use for:
   - final squad lists
   - player injuries and replacements
   - manager comments
3. Reputable reporting outlets
   Use for:
   - injury context
   - tactical previews
   - club-season form context
4. Model and simulation sites
   Use for:
   - sanity-checking probabilities
   - comparing your own shortlist against market or model consensus

Do not let a simulation site replace reasoning from current facts.

## Recommended Search Patterns

Use search patterns like:

- `site:fifa.com 2026 FIFA World Cup who has qualified`
- `site:fifa.com 2026 World Cup squad announcement <team>`
- `site:fifa.com 2026 FIFA World Cup group <team>`
- `site:fifa.com men's world ranking April 2026`
- `<federation> World Cup 2026 squad official`
- `<team> injury World Cup 2026 official`

## Evidence Checklist

Before issuing a serious forecast, try to confirm:

- exact date of the forecast
- latest official FIFA ranking window
- whether the full draw is complete
- current group opponents for the top contenders
- final squad if already announced
- material injury or suspension news
- recent serious-match form
- whether the team benefits from host geography or support

## Minimum Evidence Standard

For a quick forecast:

- 1 official tournament-format source
- 1 official ranking source
- 1 official qualified-teams or draw source
- 2-4 team-specific sources for the leading contenders

For a deeper forecast, add:

- federation squad sources
- injury confirmation
- one secondary model or market sanity check

## Common Failure Modes

- Treating old rankings as current
- Ignoring that the draw changes championship odds
- Overweighting star names without checking squad balance
- Using club form to assume automatic international fit
- Forgetting to date the prediction
